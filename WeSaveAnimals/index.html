Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a better life for adoptable companion animals without homes and to assist in the effort to limit the overpopulation of these animals through a low kill methodology. We work diligently to educate the public, especially children, on the importance of pet sterilization, responsible pet ownership and the humane treatment of all animals. We believe that by working together we can save them all.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a humane and compassionate future for every animal friend by connecting them with loving and responsible adopters. We strive to provide a digital experience that enhances the pet adoption process and improves the lives of both animals and humans.

About The Founder

Praveen Kumar L, also called Rider Praveen is an ex-gym instructor who gave up his job, his means of livelihood, all his and his family assets and savings to start We Save Animals (WSA) Welfare Trust. From paralysed, blind, old aged animals to accident cases, cruelty cases and terminally ill animals - he takes them all in, giving them happiness, food and a home in his shelter.
It started for Praveen in 2013, when he rescued a kitten with a spinal injury. He nursed it at home, but it passed away after a week, leaving Praveen to wonder what he could do to help numerous animals like this, that suffer in silence.
